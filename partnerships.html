Hotels

We offer long-term partnerships to design and operate hotel kids’ clubs end-to-end: daily creative schedules, weekend specials, and holiday programs that keep young guests happy while parents relax. We align with your brand standards, handle staffing and materials, and deliver measurable upsides in family bookings, guest satisfaction, and reviews.

Schools

Our school deals include field trips to our center for hands-on STEM & art workshops (safety briefings, supervisors, and all materials included). We also support occasional celebrations on campus—Graduation, National Day, Science Week—with mobile stations, themed activities, and stage moments tailored to your calendar.

Malls

We partner with malls for occasional entertainment shows, pop-up craft zones, and seasonal activations (Ramadan/Eid, Back-to-School, Winter, etc.). Our setups are flexible to fit atriums or corridors, boost dwell time and foot traffic, and create memorable, family-friendly experiences that keep visitors coming back.
